WHITE WATER RAFTING

A white water rafting mission typically focuses on providing a thrilling and memorable adventure experience while prioritizing safety and responsible environmental stewardship.

History

White water rafting's history spans from ancient transportation methods to modern adventure sport.
Early rafts, often made of logs or animal hides, were crucial for traversing waterways for food,
supplies, and exploration. The modern sport took shape in the mid-20th century,
with commercial rafting trips gaining popularity as an adventurous pastime.
